MY FAVORITE SHOW

The basic plot line of one piece

One peice follows the pirate that goes by the name of Monkey .D Luffy, when he was younger he ate a fruit that turned him into a man made of rubber. The story follows him gathering crewmates to become the king of the pirates.

Why it is the best

One peice is just the gift that keeps on giving. Everytime i finish a season i think, how could this get any better? And it just keeps getting better. Each season is better than the last in some way. The fact that they can make 1000+ episodes and keep getting better blows my mind. That is why GOAT piece is the best of all time. :)
